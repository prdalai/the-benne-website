Our Story

"From a small street stall to your favorite corner cafe."

What started as a humble street cart on Kolshet Road has blossomed into **the.benne**, a beloved cafe dedicated to serving authentic Bengaluru flavors.

Through hard work, passion, and an unwavering commitment to quality, Ganesh Sathe, a 23-year-old entrepreneur built a daily following by serving perfect Benne Dosas. We poured our heart into every dosa, realizing the secret lies not just in the simple ingredients, but in the honor, respect, and care invested in its making.

Now serving comfort food clocking ₹40,000 in daily sales, right here in Thane.
